Take a Look at Some of Our Work

XXX Company is a ISO/TS-16949:2009 production metal stamping facility specializing in stainless steel stampings. We manufacture primarily exhaust and trim products for the automotive industry. We also produce: wheel covers, wheel cover retention systems, brackets, body reinforcement pieces, frame members, and much more. From prototyping to large run orders... BRP has it covered.

Click on an image below to view additional photos of our products.

Decorative Trim


Big Rapids Products produces stamped and roll formed decorative trim components including: Window Moldings, Belt Line Moldings, Head Lamp Bezels, Mirror Housings, and many other Decorative Trim components.

Exhaust System Components


Big Rapids Products produces stamped exhaust manifolds, Stamped Muffler Shells, Muffler and Resonator housings and Internal Muffler Components, Catalytic Converter End Cones, “Y” Collectors, Heat Shields, Gas Diverters, and exhaust pipe Hangers.

Structural Stampings


Other stampings produced for the automotive industry include: Door Handle Components, Sun Roof Reinforcements, A B and C Pillar Reinforcements and brackets for various applications. Many of these components require secondary operations such as welding, light assembly, e-coat and plating.

Wheel Covers Overlays Retainers


Stainless Steel Wheel Covers and Overlays, Aluminum Overlays and Wheel Cover Retention Systems. Retention Systems are either formed wire components to reinforce plastic covers or spring steel stampings which snap onto lug nuts or wheel features. Two of these systems are Patented by Big Rapids Products.
